Conclusion

On the qualitative data side, Girl Groups are more profitable than other categories combined. There are many reasons why the results are like that. It could be due to the distribution of the companies of the albums that affects the equivalent quantitative side of the mentioned. Companies usually send or ship the albums out of South Korea when the primary or the majority audience of that particular group is outside of their country. The shipments are counted in Album Sales. With that information, some groups would chart well with Album Sales but not with Retail Album Sales.

The results also tell us what K-pop stans are looking for right now. Since the majority of the sales are coming from Girl Groups, we can say that today is now the “Girl Group Age”. Their charm, talents, and music captured the hearts of the people, which eventually led them to be highly profitable because they were in demand compared to the other group categories.
